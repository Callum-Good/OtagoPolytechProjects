What should I do with my electronic waste?

There are e-Waste collection facilities at the Green Island Resource Recovery Centre for computer waste and TVs. A small recycling charge applies to monitors and TVs, as they require special handling.
New Zealand doesnâ€™t have safe recycling facilities to handle computer monitors and TVs so they are sent off shore, usually to Singapore, Korea or Australia to authenticated recyclers, under hazardous waste transport Basel Permits. The charge helps pay for the ethical recycling of our e-waste.
Computer waste is dismantled by Cargill Enterprises, 199 Hillside Rd, South Dunedin. Computer waste can also be dropped off there during business hours of Monday to Friday 8am to 4.30pm. A small recycling charge applies.
