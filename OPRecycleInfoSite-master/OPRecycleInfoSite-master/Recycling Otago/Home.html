It's important to recycle correctly. Different types of rubbish go in different bins. Play this game and see if you know what rubbish goes in what bin.

To play, simply drag and drop the rubbish into the bin you think it goes in. If you get it wrong, try again. You may reset the game at any time.

Red bin:

Holds waste products, such as rubbish bags.

Blue bin:

Holds glass recycling, such as glass bottles.

Yellow bin:

Holds plastic, cans, paper and cardboard recycling like cardboard boxes.
